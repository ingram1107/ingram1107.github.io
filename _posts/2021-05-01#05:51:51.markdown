May 1st, a day to celebrate the struggle of proletariats against the capitalists
and the establishment, a day to commemorate the sacrifices made by proletariats
for the course of eliminate inequality among people. May 1st, is a day to show
solidarity among workers despite ideological differences. Anarchists or Marxists
alike, should put down their differences on this day, rally together to voice
for the same people that they sincerely care about.

We should not forget about the ongoing affairs related to working condition of
the working class. The struggle of local and migrant workers for better living
standard in Malaysia, the repression on union workers by fascists' junta regime
in Myanmar, the worsening working condition of workers in China, the continuous
indifference on the welfare of healthcare and services workers in Taiwan, the
pessimistic situation of the working class in Japan and Korea, the protests on
the Farm Bills in India, and the ongoing struggle for higher minimum wage and
stronger labour unions in US.

Cheers to those who choose to keeping the fight on, happy International Workers'
Day!
