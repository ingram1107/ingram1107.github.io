Yesterday, 01 Feb 2021, Tatmadaw, the Myanmar army, illegally detained several important political figures included President Win Myint, State Counsellor Aung San Suu Kyi, several Ministers and members of Myanmar Assembly of the Union. After this coup d'état, Tatmadaw later on imposed one year national emergency based on their fraudulent claim on Myanmar general election. These juntas claim that the election is unfair to minority ethnics despite they are the active participants of minority cleansing and repression.

The 2021 Myanmar coup d'état once again proves that revolution is a necessary measure against an authoritative regime. Aung San Suu Kyi, though I highly respect for her efforts for Myanmar democratic progress, citing her silence over the ethnic cleansing of Rohingya and constantly compromise with Myanmar fascists, beg for the survival of Myanmar civilian government. When National League for Democracy (NLD) won a landslide in the 2020 Myanmar general election, Myanmar juntas feel the threats and decided to seize power from the elected government to protect their interests.

There should be no compromises to fascists, there should be no talk with the juntas. It is the mistake to think that Tatmadaw is a friendly ally to Myanmar democratic government. All Myanmar citizens regardless of your race should be united in one banner. There should be no more sacrifices of minority's utilities to suit the need to protect Myanmar futile democracy. There is no hope for reformism under such an authoritative regime.

The message from those juntas is clear: Revolution is the only practical mean to overthrow such an unjust system. Myanmar people should mobilise the mass to resist this newly formed government. Myanmar people should take any means to destroy those juntas' efforts to "stabilise" the country. Myanmar people should be able to decide their path with their own will and blood.

I suggest that Myanmar people prepare for a national wide revolution by advocating the following measures:
1. Refuse to participate in future electoral progress
2. Spread class consciousness among the people publicly or secretly
3. Conduct protests and demonstrations on the street
4. Secretly arm yourselves

Down with the Tatmadaw!
