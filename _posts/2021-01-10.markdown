The attempt to take over the government on 6 January 2021 had failed but it delivered a great shock to the world on how inadequate the security system that US have around its most important building. Those police officers are said incompetent at best, treason at worst. There are clear evidences that the polices were working together with the rioters. \[[1][video1]\]\[[2][video2]\]

Frankly, I am not surprise that the police are in favour of those fascists during BLM (Black Lives Matter) and Antifa (Anti-fascisms) protests. When those peaceful protesters being arrested for no wrongdoing, violent groups such as Proud Boy get away from stabbing easily under the eyes of the polices. The thing that caught me off guard is that there were no one standing out against the idea to cooperate with the rioters which shows how corrupted the US police system is. A serious consequence should fall to those polices for their own inadequate and inability to withhold their biases.

After this mess, I would say it is a great time for the American socialists to radicalise their movement to sway over previously doubted liberals into our course. Socialists need to exploit this opportunity to raise the class conciousness with the absence of [Trump's twitter account][based-twitter]. Please discard the idea from Joe Biden to "heal the nation", BLM and Antifa should try to take over the street to convince the public to defund the police, call out the fascists and denounce those who in favour of reactionary acts instead. Continuing the efforts to unionise the tech workers especially within the Big FAANG (Facebook, Amazon, Apple, Netflix, Google) and other local industries. Offer mutual aid to those who in need especially during the pandemic but remember to protect yourselves from the virus.

In the long run, US socialists should work along side with social democrats like Bernie Sander, AOC (Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez) and Rashida Tlaib. Persuade the progressive liberals to pass reforms such as Medicare for All, raise on minimum wage, police reform etc. Voice out the discontents on military spending, police violences and working conditions of the working class. Remember to deplatform those alt-right and take over the voice on social media where socialists can win over the proletariat.

Wish you well, comrades, and tomorrow, L'Internationale will be the human race.
