# Capacitor's Charge and Discharge

The following examples show the equation for calculating charge and discharge of
a capacitor:

$$
\begin{align}
Q_{\text{charge}}(t) &= Q_0 (1 - e^{-\frac{t}{RC}})\\
Q_{\text{discharge}}(t) &= Q_0 e^{-\frac{t}{RC}}
\end{align}
$$
