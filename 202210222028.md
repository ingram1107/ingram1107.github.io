# Euler's Theorem

Euler's Theorem states:

$$
a^{\phi(n)} \mod n = 1
$$

Where:
- $GCD(a, n) = 1$, meaning $a$ is [relatively prime](202210092214.md)# to $n$
- $\phi$ stands for [Euler Totient Function](202210222004.md)#

It is a generalisation of [Fermat's Theorem](202210221928.md)#.
